Frequently Asked Questions

What services does Rinterio offer?

At Rinterio, we specialize in architectural design, interior design, space planning, and project management. If you're looking to design a new home, renovate an existing space, or create a unique commercial environment, our team is here to help.

What is the process for starting a new project with Rinterio?

Our process begins with an initial consultation where we discuss your vision, requirements, and budget. After that, we will create a design proposal.

How do I book a consultation with Rinterio?

You can easily book a consultation through our website by clicking the "Book Consult" button at the top of the page. Alternatively, you can contact us directly via phone or email to schedule a meeting at your convenience.

What is the cost of your services?

The cost of our services varies depending on the project type and scope.

Do I need to be involved in the design process?

While we handle all aspects of the design process, we highly value your input and encourage collaboration. Your involvement ensures that the final design aligns with your vision and meets your expectations.
